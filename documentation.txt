Business - Small grocery, clothes, bakery shop.

Domains - Customer, supplier, product, sale, purchase, payment, expense.

Workflows -
    1. Sale Workflow -
        customer give product list -->
        owner give goods -->
        customer give payment -->
        sales recorded and stock decrease

    2. Purchase Workflow -
        owner order goods to suppliers -->
        suppliers send good through transportation -->
        owner recieve good -->
        owner give payment to transporter -->
        expense recorded -->
        owner give payment to supplier -->
        purchase recorded and stock increase

Requirement - 
    1. track payment record
    2. track sales
    3. handle inventory
    4. track purchase
    5. have customer and suppliers details
    6. show summary of data
    7. track expenses

Data Model -
    1. customer -- id, name, recipt type, payment(online/cash)
    2. supplier -- id, name, contact number, payment(online/cash)
    3. inventory --id, product name, quantity, price, category
    4. expense -- id place, money 
    6. sale -- id, product, quantity, price
    7. purchase -- id, product, quantity, price
    7. payment -- id, product, quantity, price

System Design -
    1. dashboard module-- total stock, stock quantity, today sale, today purchase, low stock, out of stock
    2. inventory module-- product name, quantity, price, category, indicator(in stock/low stock/stock out), CRUD
    3. customer module-- name, payment type, recipt type, CRUD
    4. supplier module-- name, contact number, payment type, CRUD
    5. sale module-- invoice, payment in, CRUD
    6. purchase module-- bill, payment out, CRUD
    7. expense module-- place, money, name, CRUD
